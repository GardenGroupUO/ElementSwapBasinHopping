Installation
------------

To use, place into your ~/.bashrc:

###################################
########## Basin Hopping ##########
export PATH_TO_BH="<path-to-BasinHopping_v-folder>"

export PYTHONPATH="$PATH_TO_BH":$PYTHONPATH

###################################

For information how to use this, look at:

https://wiki.fysik.dtu.dk/ase/ase/optimize.html#module-ase.optimize.basin
